About Me

WELCOME!!! My name is Jessie. I am visually impaired and have been using screen readers to navigate the computer for the past 20 years. I have used many websites throughout my professional career and have seen some online platforms which are very accessible and some which are more challenging to navigate. My interest in web accessibility and how to make the internet a more comprehensive space has fueled my desire to become an accessibility consultant. My goal is to help individuals, companies, and small businesses meet WCAG 2.2 compliance while also inspecting the accessibility of the website as a human tester. I have been developing my skillsets to be an excellent consultant and advocate for accessibility in the online space and am dedicated to find solutions for you and your online platforms. I look forward to working with you!

Below is a brief list of my professional experiences and relevant training programs:

  • Completed the DHS Web tester program with a score of over 80%.
  • 100 Devs Boot Camp (in progress).
  • Assistive technology instructor for the blind/visually impaired.
    • Chinese/English interpreter.

My commitment to growth in this field means that I am continuously learning, training, and finding new ways to approach problems in the world of accessibility.
