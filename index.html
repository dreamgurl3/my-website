About Me

WELCOME!!! My name is Jessie. I am visually impaired myself and have been using Screen Reader to navigate the computer for the past 20 years. I visited many websites throughout my life. I have seen some websites are very accessible and some are more challenging to navigate due to lack of labels or other issues. Therefore, I have a desire to become an accessible consultant to help anyone who like to know if their website is not only complance to WCAG 2.2 but also accessible with human tester. I took the DHS Web Accessibility Training program and achieve a score of over 80%. I also learned how to develop myself to be a better consultant.
